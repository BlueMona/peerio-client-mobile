Content Wrappers

Content wrappers are specially positioned divs designed to work on mobile webviews. They are absolutely positioned and have values for all top bottom left right properties. This ensures they are always positioned at the maximum width and height of the viewport.

Content wrappers inherit from the content-wrapper mixin.

Content wrappers have modifier classes for special scenarios, like .content-wrapper.with-reply-box and content-wrapper.without-tab-bar.
